# Business Case — E-Commerce Delivery Analytics

## Business Problem

Brazilian marketplace leadership wants to understand declining customer satisfaction and delayed deliveries. Which delivery and seller factors drive lower review scores? Which regions have the highest delay rates? Can we identify actionable seller segments responsible for systemic underperformance?

## Dataset & Methodology

**Source:** Olist Brazilian E-Commerce Public Dataset (Kaggle)  
**Scope:** ~100,000 orders across 9 relational tables, covering 2016–2018  
**Analytical approach:**

1. Descriptive — establish baseline: review score distribution, delivery rate, delay frequency
2. Correlation — quantify the relationship between delivery lag and review score
3. Segmentation — identify geographic and seller-level patterns
4. Recommendation — translate findings into prioritized operational actions

## Hypotheses

- **H1:** Orders delivered after the estimated delivery date receive systematically lower review scores
- **H2:** Specific Brazilian states or regions have disproportionately higher delay rates
- **H3:** A segment of sellers accounts for a disproportionate share of late deliveries and negative reviews
